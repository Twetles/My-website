My Hobbies & Interests

Discover what kind of Music, Anime, Video Games, Physical Activities, and many other pieces of media I enjoy in my free time!

Valorant

Valorant is a team-based first-person tactical hero shooter set in the near future. Players play as one of a set of Agents, characters based on several countries and cultures around the world. In the main game mode with the 4 agent types (Duelist, Sentinel, Initiator, and Controller) players are assigned to either the attacking or defending team with each team having five players on it.

Genshin Impact

The player takes on the role of the Traveler, an interstellar adventurer who is separated from their twin sibling after landing in Teyvat. The Traveler must journey across the seven nations of Teyvat to find their lost sibling, accompanied by their guide, Paimon. Along the way, the Traveler will befriend many people, become involved in the nations' affairs, and unravel the mysteries of the land.

Honkai Star Rail

Players embark on an interstellar journey aboard the Astral Express, a train that travels across the universe. You play as the Trailblazer, a character implanted with a powerful and mysterious entity called a Stellaron. Stellaron, also known as "the Cancer of All Worlds," causes chaos and destruction in the worlds it affects. Your mission is to uncover the mysteries of the Stellaron, explore diverse planets, and resolve the crises plaguing each world.
